Shridhar Developers

Shridhar Developers began with a simple purpose — to create spaces where people feel truly at home. In 2008, with years of experience already behind us, we put our first brick in place — not just for a building, but for a belief. A belief that a home means more than four walls. It means peace, safety, and that proud smile on your face when you say, "Yeh ghar mera hai." Since then, we’ve completed 25 projects, delivered over 15 lakh sq. ft., and have 9 lakh sq. ft. under construction. But honestly, those numbers? They’re not what stay with us. What we remember is the warm ‘thank you’ from a family, the tears in someone’s eyes seeing their new home for the first time, the trust people place in us when they hand over their life’s savings. We still believe in doing the right thing, even if it’s not the easiest. We build like we’re building for our own — with care, emotion, and full responsibility. Shridhar Developers is part of a close-knit group of companies guided by the same values. With Shridhar Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. as the mother company, and sister firms like Siddhartha Maison and Sagun Ishaan Infra Developers Pvt. Ltd., we continue working together toward one heartfelt goal — to build not just houses, but places people proudly call home. At the end of the day, we’re not just builders. We’re people who’ve felt what you feel — and that’s why we care so much. Shridhar Developers Built with belief. Delivered with heart.

About the Founder

Upendra Singh Born on April 30, 1975, in Gaya, Bihar fondly known as Chikku Singh—has followed a path defined by purpose and resilience. A graduate and former officer of the Indian Navy, he transitioned from national service to the business world with a mission: help people own a home. He began in garments, moved into hospitality, and eventually stepped into real estate in 2008 with a deep personal connection to the housing struggle. His first project in Harmu, Ranchi, laid the groundwork for what is now 24 completed developments and 5 ongoing projects across 25+ years. Under his leadership, Shridhar Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. became the anchor of a group of real estate companies that include Shridhar Developers, Siddhartha Maison, and Sagun Ishaan Infra Developers Pvt. Ltd.—all sharing values of integrity and trust. His journey isn’t just business. It’s personal. Every home built is a promise kept. Today, he continues to lead with heart, shaping lives and skylines with the same focus: work that’s lived, not just delivered.
